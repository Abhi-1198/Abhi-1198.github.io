Important directions:

  • The author presenting the second and subsequent papers will get 30% discount, if he/she registers with the same author name and e-mail ID.
  • Friends and relatives of the Indian delegates can attend the conference sessions, refreshments and lunch by paying a nominal fee of Rs. 2500/- per person only. However, they will not be provided any conference kit, participation or presentation certificate.
  • Foreign delegates can accompany anyone in the conference by paying a nominal fee of 150 USD per person only. They will not be given any conference kit, participation, or presentation certificates.
  • Co-authors must register by paying full registration fees, in case they want to attend and get presentation certificate as a conference delegate.

Registration Fee include:

  • Conference kit
  • Tea/Coffee
  • Lunch
  • Grand Conference Dinner
  • Conference Proceedings/CD
  • Registration fees does not include Accommodation and Transportation fees
