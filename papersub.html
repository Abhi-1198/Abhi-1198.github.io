Call for Papers

Faculty members/ research scholars/ scientists from India are invited to submit original unpublished research in the topics mentioned below. Research papers should present novel perspectives within the general scope of the conference. The paper length should not exceed 08 pages. All submitted papers will be subjected to a "Plagiarism check" by Turnitin Software. Papers achieving less than 20% similarity score will only be sent for the formal review. To submit the manuscript, the authors are requested to use the easy chair link. Guidelines for the preparation of the full-length paper and online submission will be soon provided on the conference website.
